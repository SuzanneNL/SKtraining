About SK Training

The best way to learn a language is to speak it. At SK Training, we focus on teaching our students Dutch in such a way, that they can use it immediately! SK Training is a Dutch language school that offers courses, levels A0 to B2, either in private or in small groups, either at home or in our classroom in the OBA. We are also available for in-company training. We will come to your company or are at your disposal in the OBA. SK Training can set up a course tailored to your employers' needs. For example, we can help them to improve their understanding of safety measures. Or we can improve their professional writing skills. Please have a look at our work history to see the companies we've provided training for, or fill out the contact form for more information and/or to request a free quote.

About Sanne Kroon

My name is Sanne Kroon. I founded SK Training in 2017. I have a background in linguistics and Spanish, and am a certified NT2 teacher. Teaching is my passion, because I love looking back at my students' successes and telling them: “Look at that! You did that!” The combination of teaching what's relevant and showing a student his or her progress, is what makes my method a success!
